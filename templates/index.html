AI Systems

Applied AI — Not Just Experiments

Multi-LLM Research System

Built a system that routes queries across multiple LLM providers to compare responses, extract structured insights, and reduce hallucination risk. Designed for experimentation, evaluation, and workflow integration.

AI Academic Assistant

A document-aware assistant designed to help students analyze academic material, summarize readings, and extract key concepts from PDFs and notes. Focused on practical usage rather than chatbot novelty.

Custom Chatbot Implementations

Developed multiple task-focused chatbot systems for structured Q&A, workflow assistance, and guided interaction — built as modular wrappers over modern LLM APIs rather than generic chat interfaces.
